# Foundation Worksheets

You can't *be the answer* if you don't know the question. These three worksheets build your foundation —
do them in order. Fill them in by hand, or work through them with AI (each links to a matching prompt).

**Go slow here, to go fast later.**

| # | Worksheet | The question it answers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Understand Your Customers](1-understand-your-customers.md) | Who are they, and what do they need? |
| 2 | [Understand Yourself](2-understand-yourself.md) | Who are you to them, and how will they find you? |
| 3 | [Your Value Proposition](3-value-proposition.md) | Say it in one sentence |

When you're done, you'll have everything you need to fill in your
[Brand Context](../../prompts/04-brand-context.md) so AI sounds like *you*.
