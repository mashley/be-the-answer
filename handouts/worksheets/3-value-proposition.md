# Worksheet 3 — Your Value Proposition

*Foundation, part 3 of 3. Everything you just learned, in one sentence.*

> **Why this matters:** if you can't say what you do in one clear sentence, your customer can't either —
> and a confused customer never buys. This is the sentence your whole brand hangs on.

---

## Fill in the blanks

> **My product** `______________________________________`
>
> **helps** *(target customer)* `______________________________________`
>
> **do** *(what)* `______________________________________`
>
> **so that** *(benefit)* `______________________________________`

## Now write it as one sentence

`_____________________________________________________________________`

`_____________________________________________________________________`

**Example:** *"SuperBackpack helps college students carry their books, snacks, and notepads to class so
that they don't hurt their back."*

---

## Optional — sharpen it against the competition

> **Unlike** *(the competition)* `______________________________________`,
>
> **my product** *(does A, B, C)* `______________________________________`,
>
> **which means** *(your real advantage)* `______________________________________`.

---

**The test:** read your sentence to someone who doesn't know your business. If they can repeat back what
you do and who it's for — it works. If they look confused — shrink it and try again.

🤖 **With AI:** paste your draft into [Prompt 3 — Be the Answer](../../prompts/03-be-the-answer-content.md)
or use it to seed your [Brand Context](../../prompts/04-brand-context.md).
