## 1. The 5-minute recap

**Marketing's real job** is to bring real people in and turn them into customers — not to make pretty
pictures. And the way people find you has changed: your customer now asks an **AI** and gets *one answer*.
If it's not you, you're invisible — and you'll never know. So your job is to **be the answer.**

**You can't be the answer if you don't know the question.** So start with your customer. Then follow them
through the **customer journey** — four stages, each a different question you answer:

| Stage | Their question | Your content's job |
|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | "Does a solution like this exist?" | Be seen; name the problem |
| **Acquisition** | "Why should I choose you?" | Help them decide (honestly) |
| **Adoption** | "How do I get the most out of this?" | Help them succeed |
| **Advocacy** | "How do I tell others?" | Make sharing easy |

**AI lets one person do the work of a whole team** — but only if you give it your *context* (who you are,
your customer, your voice). AI without context makes junk, faster.

## 2. Do this today in Claude (free — 20 minutes)

Open [claude.ai](https://claude.ai), and run these [copy-paste prompts](../prompts/) in order:

1. **[Know your customer](../prompts/01-know-your-customer.md)** — who actually pays, and what they need.
2. **[Build your brand context](../prompts/04-brand-context.md)** — so AI sounds like *you*. **(Do this early — everything else uses it.)**
3. **[Find the questions](../prompts/02-find-the-questions.md)** — the real questions your customers ask.
4. **[Be the answer](../prompts/03-be-the-answer-content.md)** — turn one question into a real post.
5. **[AI visibility check](../prompts/05-ai-visibility-check.md)** — does AI know you exist? How to fix it.
6. **[Create once, radiate everywhere](../prompts/06-create-once-radiate-everywhere.md)** — one idea → many posts.

Want Claude to remember your brand every time, without pasting? Load a **[Skill](../skills/)**
(`be-the-answer` and `brand-voice-builder`) — the [skills page](../skills/) shows you how in 2 minutes.
