# Prompt 4 — Build Your Brand Context

**Use for:** the most important prompt. AI without context makes junk. This builds a reusable "about my
brand" block so every future post sounds like *you*, not a generic robot.

## Prompt

```text
You are a brand strategist. Interview me to build a reusable "Brand Context" I can
paste into any AI so it always sounds like me.

Ask me these one at a time, then write the finished Brand Context:
1. What do you do, and who for? (one sentence)
2. What promise do you make to customers?
3. Who is your ideal customer? (the person who pays)
4. What are their top 3 pains?
5. What makes you different / better / cheaper / faster?
6. 3 words that describe your voice (e.g. direct, warm, no hype)
7. One thing you should NEVER sound like
8. Your main offer and the action you want people to take

Then output a clean, copy-pasteable "BRAND CONTEXT" block I can reuse. Keep it tight.
```

## Notes
- Save the output. Paste it at the top of any content prompt (like [Prompt 3](03-be-the-answer-content.md))
  and the AI will sound like you.
