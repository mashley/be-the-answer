# Prompt 6 — Create Once, Radiate Everywhere

**Use for:** you made one good piece of content. Now turn it into many, shaped for each channel — without
starting over each time.

## Prompt

```text
You are a content repurposing expert. Each social channel is different, so the same
idea needs a different shape for each.

My brand context: [PASTE FROM PROMPT 4]
The content I already made: [PASTE YOUR POST / BLOG / TRANSCRIPT / IDEA]

Turn it into:
- A LinkedIn post (professional, a clear insight, no outside link in the body —
  put any link in the first comment)
- An Instagram caption (warmer, first line is a hook before the "more" cutoff)
- An X / Twitter post (under 200 characters, one sharp idea, no hashtag stuffing)
- A short email (subject line + 3 short paragraphs)
- 3 ideas for a short video (just the hook + the point)

Keep my voice consistent across all of them. Don't invent facts. Don't use hype.
```

## Notes
- One transcript of a real conversation can become a week of content. Record a good talk, drop the
  transcript in here, and go.
- This is the whole idea behind **Radi8** — *create once, radiate everywhere* — and it can post to your
  channels for you. See the [Start Here handout](../handouts/start-here.md).
