# Prompt 2 — Find the Questions

**Use for:** you can only *be the answer* if you know the questions. This finds the real questions your
customers ask at each stage of their journey.

## Prompt

```text
You are a customer-research expert who understands the customer journey:
Awareness → Acquisition → Adoption → Advocacy.

My customer is: [PASTE YOUR IDEAL CUSTOMER FROM PROMPT 1]
What I sell: [PRODUCT OR SERVICE]

For EACH of the four stages, list the real questions this customer is asking —
the way they would actually type or say them (to a friend, to Google, or to an AI):

- Awareness (they don't know I exist): "does something like this exist?" type questions
- Acquisition (comparing options): "why should I choose you?" type questions
- Adoption (they bought — are they succeeding?): "how do I get the most out of this?" questions
- Advocacy (they love it): "how do I tell others / who else needs this?" questions

Give me 5 real questions per stage. Then mark the 3 questions overall that,
if I answered them well in public, would bring me the most customers.
```

## Notes
- Each question is a piece of content waiting to be made. Feed the best ones into
  [Prompt 3 — Be the Answer](03-be-the-answer-content.md).
- Bonus: paste these questions into ChatGPT/Perplexity and see who it recommends as the answer today.
  That's your [AI visibility check](05-ai-visibility-check.md).
