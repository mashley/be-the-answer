# Prompts — copy, paste, be the answer

Beginner-friendly prompts you can paste straight into **Claude** (claude.ai) or **ChatGPT**. No setup
needed. Each one is a small tool for a real marketing job. Start at the top and work down.

| # | Prompt | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Know your customer](01-know-your-customer.md) | Figure out who actually pays you, and what they need |
| 2 | [Find the questions](02-find-the-questions.md) | List the real questions your customers ask, stage by stage |
| 3 | [Be the answer](03-be-the-answer-content.md) | Turn one customer question into content that answers it |
| 4 | [Build your brand context](04-brand-context.md) | Create a reusable "about my brand" block so AI sounds like *you* |
| 5 | [AI visibility check](05-ai-visibility-check.md) | See if AI knows you exist, and how to get recommended |
| 6 | [Create once, radiate everywhere](06-create-once-radiate-everywhere.md) | Turn one piece of content into many |

**How to use:** copy a prompt, paste it into Claude, and replace the `[BRACKETS]` with your details.
Do #1 and #4 first — they give every other prompt the context it needs to sound like you.
