# Prompt 5 — AI Visibility Check (AEO)

**Use for:** finding out whether AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) know you exist — and how
to become the answer they give. This is AEO: Answer Engine Optimization.

## Do this first (by hand, free — 5 minutes)
Open ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. Ask each one the questions your customer would ask, e.g.:
- "Who is the best [what you do] for [your customer / place]?"
- "What are the top options for [the problem you solve]?"

Note: **Do you appear? Who appears instead? What did it say about them?**

## Then paste this prompt

```text
You are an AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) analyst.

My business: [WHAT YOU DO, FOR WHOM]
My website: [URL]
The questions my customers ask AI: [PASTE 3 QUESTIONS FROM PROMPT 2]
When I asked AI these questions, it recommended: [PASTE WHO SHOWED UP, IF ANYONE]

Do this:
1. Explain, in plain words, why those competitors are showing up and I'm not.
2. List the specific places (directories, publications, review sites, communities)
   where a business like mine should be listed and cited so AI trusts it.
3. Give me a prioritized 5-step action plan to become the answer AI recommends —
   easiest and highest-impact first.
4. Suggest 3 pieces of content I could publish this month that AI would want to cite.
```

## The one idea to remember
**The more trusted places point to you, the more the AI trusts you.** Get written about, get listed, get
reviews, and answer real questions in public. Be genuinely, consistently useful.
